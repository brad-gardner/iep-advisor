Our mission

Every Parent Deserves to Understand
Their Child's IEP

The IEP process is supposed to be a partnership between families and schools. But too often, parents are handed a dense, jargon-filled document and expected to understand — and advocate — without the tools or knowledge they need.

IEP Advisor was created to change that. We believe that when parents truly understand their child's IEP, they become the most powerful advocates their children will ever have.

"We're building the tool we wish every parent had from day one."
